Installation Guide

1.Download and install/run the client.

2.Provide your ISO of Jak & Daxter to the client when prompted.

2.5.If you intend to do speedruns, create a user in the client.

Getting Started

1.Open the client and navigate to the lobby page.

2.Create or join a game/lobby.

NOTE!Previous OpenGOAL instances will close when entering a new lobby!

3.Join a team unless you intend to spectate.

4.Launch the game from either the "LAUNCH" button or the hamburger menu in the top left.

5.Ready up when you're ready.

Spectator Mode Controls

Left/Right- Change player.

R3- Toggle freecam.

Up- Toggle look-at camera.

Down- Refocus camera on player.

R2 (held)- Faster freecam camera.

R1 (held)- Move freecam up.

L1 (held)- Move freecam down.
